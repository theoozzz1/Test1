We use both session and persistent Cookies for the purposes set out below:

\begin{itemize}

	\begin{itemize}
		\item Type: Session Cookies
		\item Administered by: Us
		\item Purpose: These Cookies are essential to provide You with services available through the Website and to enable You to use some of its features.
	\end{itemize}
	\item \textbf{Functionality Cookies}
	\begin{itemize}
		\item Type: Persistent Cookies
		\item Administered by: Us
		\item Purpose: These Cookies allow Us to remember choices You make when You use the Website, such as remembering your login details or language preference.
	\end{itemize}
\end{itemize}

\chapter{Allergens Notice}
\textit{Updated 15/02/26 in line with Natasha's Law (2021) and the Food Information Regulations Act (2014)}
\bigskip

\section*{Byte Me allergens notice}
Our bundles will always note if they may contain any of the \textbf{14 major allergens}. This may be as a known ingredient or if there is a risk of cross contamination. The fourteen major allergens are as follows:

\begin{enumerate}
	\item Celery
	\item Cereals containing gluten
	\item Crustaceans
	\item Eggs
	\item Fish
	\item Lupin
	\item Milk
	\item Molluscs
	\item Mustard
	\item Tree nuts
	\item Peanuts
	\item Sesame seeds
	\item Soya
	\item Sulphites with 10mg/kg concentration or higher
\end{enumerate}

Any other allergens may not be explicitly listed and \textbf{must} be checked yourself. For any enquiries visit our support page.
